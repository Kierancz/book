About Kluster


FAQ:

How can you collaborate?

Kluster allows you to add anyone to your collections as a collaborator so that you don't have to do all the work.

What's the ideal number of collaborators?

While there's no limit to the collaborators, we find collections do best anywhere from 2-10 people.

What skills do I need to start collecting?

No technical skills required, we take care of all the embed code for your collection items so all you need to know is how to copy and paste URLs. Although we do offer advanced options for your items.

Is this supposed to be fun? or just practical?

We hope this can be both fun and practical! It's a really fun way to discover new media from your friends and it's very practical to save resources for viewing later.

How can I see what my friends are doing?

New collections and items added by your friends appear in real-time and will be marked as new. You may also set your preferences to be notified when your collaborators make changes.

What do admin priveledges give you?

Admins can delete offensive collections/items and can view and edit users.
